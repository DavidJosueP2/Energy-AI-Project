Abstract
  Genetic algorithm (GA) is an adaptive search technique based on natural selection and genetic rules. GA takes place in
various scienti c applications and it can be also used to determine the membership functions in a fuzzy system. The aim
of the study is to adjust the shape of membership functions and a novice aspect of the study is to determine the kind of
membership functions. Proposed method has been implemented using a developed GA program for a single input and output
fuzzy system. c 2001 Elsevier Science B.V. All rights reserved.
